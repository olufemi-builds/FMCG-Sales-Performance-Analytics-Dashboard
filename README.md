# FMCG Sales Performance Dashboard

## Overview
This project analyzes FMCG sales data to evaluate product performance, regional trends, and profitability.

It simulates real-world retail analytics used in consumer goods companies for business decision making.

---

## Objectives
- Track sales and profit performance
- Identify top and low-performing products
- Analyze regional sales differences
- Evaluate customer segments
- Support business reporting with dashboards

---

## Dataset
- 5,000 synthetic FMCG transactions
- Products: Hygiene, Baby, Beauty categories
- Regions: Africa-focused retail distribution
- Metrics: Revenue, Profit, Quantity Sold

---

## KPIs
- Total Revenue
- Total Profit
- Profit Margin
- Average Order Value
- Sales Volume

---

## Tools Used
- Power BI
- Excel
- Python (data generation)

---

## Dashboard Features
- Executive KPI summary
- Product performance analysis
- Regional sales breakdown
- Customer segmentation insights

---

## Key Insights
- A small number of products drive majority revenue
- Regional performance varies significantly
- Beauty and Hygiene dominate sales contribution
- Profit margins differ across product lines
